Places to Visit

Phnom Penh

If you took a direct fly to Cambodia. You may land either at Phnom Penh or Siem Reap International Airport. If you choose to land in Phnom Penh, it is worth to spend several days here as there are many places to visit in Phnom Penh.

City Center
- Royal Palace
- Independent Monument
- Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum
- National Museum
Outskirt
- Kirirom National Park
- Tamao Mountain
- Choeung Ek Genocide Center
Nightlife
- Sora Skybar
- The Big Easy Phnom Penh
- Club Love
Shopping
- City Mall
- EAON Mall
- Sorya Mall
Souvenir Shopping
- Tuol Tumpoung Market (Russian Market)
- Phnom Penh Night Market

More information of tour booking can be found here.

Battambang

After spending a few days in Phnom Penh, you can take a bus to the Northwestern part of Cambodia. Battambang should be should be your next stop to stay overnight or spend another one or two days here. Here, you can explore many French Colonial style Buidlings, bamboo train ride, and watching bats flying out of the cave. You can book your tour here.

Siem Reap

After Battambang, you can take a bus to Siem Reap which is wellknown for UNESCO World Heritage, Angkor temples (Angkor Wat). You may spend three or four days here as there are many ancient temples age almost 900 years old to be explored. These include:

- Bayon Temple
- Banteay Srey Temple
- Koh Ker temple
- Preah Khan temple

You can book your tours and guides here.

Preah Vihear

After touring all the ancient temples in Siem Reap, you should visit Preah Vihear province as it has an amazing temple called Preah Vihear. Preah Vihear was built in the 6th century during Khmer Empire period (wikipedia). It has the most stunning view from the top of the temple.

More information related to tour package in Preah Vihear can be found here.

Sihanoukville

Now that you have toured all the ancient temples and French colonial style buildings, you may want to have a completely new experience, somthing that is not about temples.

After returing to Phnom Penh from Siem Reap, you should take a bus or train to Sihanoukville where everything is about beaches, islands and seafoods as it is a coastal area.

Sihanoukville is also known as Kampong Som province. It locates at south-western side of the country.

Beaches
- Otres Beach
- Independence Beach
- Ochheuteal Beach
Islands
- Koh Rong
- Koh Pos (Snake Island)
