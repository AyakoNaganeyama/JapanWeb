Japan's signature food

When you hear Japanese food, Ramen and Sushi would be the first ones that pop up in your mind!

Ramen

People are obsessed about Ramen in Japan!

Read More

Ramen is one of the most popular noodle soup dish in Japan. Japan has regional varieties of Ramen and each of them has its own flavour.

Sushi

Sushi is Japanese signature dish!

Read More

In Japan, Nigiri type (slices of raw fish on oblong sushi rice) is most popular. Making sushi is really complex despite of its simple look, it takes often takes 10 years to become a skilled sushi chef.
