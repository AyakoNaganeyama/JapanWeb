Welcome to Cambodia!

Cambodia has the shape of a rabbit on the World Map as highlight in red on the map. It situates in Southeast Asia, bordered by Thailand, Laos and Vietnam. The official name for Cambodia is Kingdom of Cambodia; However, it is also called as Kampuchea. Phnom Penh is the capital city and the larget city of Cambodia.

The population of Cambodia is around 17 million. The following table summarizes the population break down by key cities with special places to visit.
