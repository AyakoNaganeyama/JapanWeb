Places to visit in Japan

There are so many amazing places in Japan! It's impossible to introduce all the places in one website, but we have chosen some of the best places to visit! One trip is probably not enough to fully enjoy Japan.

Tokyo

Tokyo Sky Tree

Tokyo Sky Tree is one of the landmark in Japan!

Read More

Tokyo Sky Tree is the tallest building in Japan. You can see an epic view of Tokyo city from height of 350 meters, and also 450 meters. If you are lucky and the weather is good, you can see Mt. Fuji
